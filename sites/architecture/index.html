The Studio

Design Rooted
in Place

Forma Design Studio was founded in 2014 by principal architect Arvind Pillai in Tirunelveli, Tamil Nadu. Our practice is shaped by a belief that architecture should respond honestly to climate, culture, and craft.

We work across scales — from intimate residences to large-scale commercial campuses — always with the same commitment to spatial quality and long-term thinking. Every project begins with listening: to the site, the client, and the community it will serve.

How We Work

The Process

A rigorous and collaborative methodology that keeps you informed and engaged at every stage.

01

Discovery & Brief

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your vision, needs, site constraints, and budget. A detailed project brief is agreed upon before any design work begins.

02

Concept Design

Schematic drawings, 3D massing models, and material mood boards are presented. We refine until the concept resonates completely with the brief.

03

Technical Design & Approvals

Detailed construction drawings, structural coordination, and all necessary municipal approvals are handled by our team so you can focus on decisions, not paperwork.

04

Construction & Delivery

On-site supervision at critical milestones ensures the built work matches the designed intent. We coordinate with contractors and inspect quality at every stage.
