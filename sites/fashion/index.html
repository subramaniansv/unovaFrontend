Our Story

Fashion with Intention

Founded in 2018, ÉLARA was born from a belief that every wardrobe deserves intention. We source premium fabrics from across the globe — Italian silks, Japanese cotton voile, Indian handlooms — and translate them into timeless pieces that live beyond seasons.

Our atelier in Tirunelveli serves clients from across South India, offering a deeply personal approach to dressing — one that begins with listening and ends with a garment that feels entirely your own.
