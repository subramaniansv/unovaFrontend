Our Story

From a Kitchen to a Movement

VERDANA was born in a small kitchen in Tirunelveli, where our founder Priya began blending her grandmother's Ayurvedic recipes with modern cosmetic science. What started as personal care for family became a passion for natural wellness shared with thousands.

Today, every product still carries that same kitchen spirit — small batches, personal attention, and an unwavering commitment to purity. We believe skincare should be honest, effective, and kind to the planet.
